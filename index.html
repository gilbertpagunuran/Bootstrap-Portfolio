About Me

I completed my college course work in the Philippines with B.S. Computer Engineering degree and have more than 20 years of professional work experience since – mostly in development functionalities. In my early years, I decided to stay in doing the gritty work of software development – which is coding. I thought coding is fun and challenging and getting paid for something I enjoy doing naturally is a dream job.

I started my career working for a consulting company that develops and deploy software overseas. It proved to be a good enterprise for it exposed me to places I never dreamed of visiting in my own time. I have worked in the shipping, banking, brokerage and finance, pharmaceutical, health insurance and utility industries in different continents. My work as a developer, technical designer and production support staff gave me different perspectives in working as a team player.

Flash forward 2017. I’m raising a family - 2 kids of my own and 1 adopted boy from the Philippines. I enjoy cooking, playing tennis and running. Earned a black belt in Tae Kwon Do with my son. Took piano lessons with my daughter. And now learning a new thing they call Full-Stack Web Development before reaching the end of my 50th year on this planet.

Why web development? Besides piquing my curiosity, the idea of retooling my skillset for my next unknown adventure is exciting! Also, I wanted to test my brain’s elasticity and improve it. I thought this is a good investment for my personal growth. Coming from relational database world, I’d like to know and understand how is such a file system can be accessed without SQL! I have been sleeping under the currents of computing. Maybe if I learn these tools, I can finally make some of my ideas into reality…
